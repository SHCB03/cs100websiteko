Raphael Laurence N. Alagaban

Raphael Laurence N. Alagaban is a first year Collage student studying at Sacred Heart Collage,
Lucena City. Part of BSCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science) is the study of
concepts and theories, algorithmic foundations, implementation and application of information
and computing solutions. It is the study of Information and Computer Technology, both
hardware and software. The Computer Science Program at Sacred Heart College is not
just devoted teaching the latest technology. The program prepares students to be IT
Professionals and researchers, and to be proficient in designing and developing computing
solutions.
